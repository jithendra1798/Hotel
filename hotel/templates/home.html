Hotel Transylvania embodies both elegance and the authenticity of the area itself. Rich wooden floors, a staircase that hugs the wall and opens to a walkway that encircles the lower living room, a sparkling pool and plenty of outdoor seating — these are just a few of the qualities that make this home fit for anyone hoping to really live the good life while vacationing.

Bar and Restaurent

Hotel Transylvania has a sumptuous bar-restaurant with indoor and outdoor seating plus an elevated, open-sided terrace under canvas. Its a great place for a lazy afternoon drink and snack, serving beer, wine and cocktails, The lunch and dinner menus features Indian and multicuisine meals, such as pizza, pasta and sliders.

Private garden

The 1878 garden will make you forget all about the city with its three-story pagoda, gourd-shaped pond and roaring waterfalls. It turns pink with 20 varieties of cherry blossoms and camellias (the hotel is known for the flower, which has been growing there in abundance since the 14th century) in the spring, and it transforms into fiery red, orange and gold in the autumn
